Chemex

Making coffee in Chemex is my absolute favor favorite way to make coffee in the world. I think it is the most awesome way to make coffee, for yourself or for a few people at once.

Ingredients

  • Coffee grinds; 28grams, mediun-coarse ground
  • Water, at 204 degrees F, 780g

how to make this coffee

  1. Freshly grind 28 grams of coffee, to a medium coarse grind
  2. Heat 900+ of water to be 204 degrees F. After heating, you will have at least 780g.
  3. Place a paper filter into the Chemex
  4. Use the heated water to wet the paper filter
  5. Discard water used to wet paper
  6. Place grinds in filter
  7. Pour 75g of water onto the grinds inn a concentric manner over the course of 30 seconds
  8. Let coffee bloom for 45 seconds.
  9. Pour 705g more of water over 3minutes30seconds in a concentric manner.
